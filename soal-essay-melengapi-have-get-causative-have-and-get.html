Causative adl kata kerja yang digunakan untuk menyuruh orang lain, causative bisa disebut juga command,

4. Causative have and get exercise I. Change the verb in the brackets info suitable for me 1. The boy has repair motorcycle 2. Anton has got HOS friend (return) the books to the library.


CAUSATIVE VERBS

1. The boy has his motorcycle [repaired].

2. Anton has got HOS friend [to return] the books to the library.

EXPLANATION

A causative verb is the verb used to explain the idea “M causes N“ to do something by requesting, persuading, paying and forcing someone.

A. [Have]

It reveals the statement “M asks/requests N" do something

Pattern :

Structure I

S+has(any tense)+someone+Verb¹.......

Exp.

The boy has someone repair his motorcycle

➠ The boy begs of someone to repair his motorcycle.

Structure II

S+has(any tense)+something+verb³....

Exp.

The boy has his motorcycle repaired.

➠ The boy requests someone for the specific reason, repairing his motorcycle. That someone doesn't have any choices to refuse it because that is part of his job.

B. [Get]

It reveals the statement “M persuades N" to do something.

Pattern

Structure I

S+get(any tense)+someone+infinitive...

Exp.

Anton has got HOS friend to return the books to the library.

➠ Anton finally tried to persuade HOS friend to return the books to the library.

Structure II

S+get(any tense)+something+verb³....

➠ Anton has got the books returned to the library.

Note :

Please remember, if we want to imply the causative verb construction, pay attention to the object.

19. Buat la kalimat causative have aktif dan passive


Subject : English

Category : Causative  

Level : SHS

Code : 5

Keywords : causative, passive, have

------------

We use causative verb to talk about an action that is done by someone else for us or for another person.  The subject of the sentence caused the action to happen but they paid/told/ordered someone else to do it.

There are two kinds of causative. The active causative and the passive causative.

Active causative :  

Let/make/have someone do something (Let/make/have + direct object + bare infinitive + indirect object)

get someone to do something (get + direct object + to infinitive + indirect object)

Passive causative :

Let/make/have/get something done. (Let/make/have + indirect object + past participle)

---------------

Examples of active causative with the verb have :

1. I have the maid mop the floor.

2. She has the hairdresser dye her hair.

3. My mother had the man carry her shopping.

4. The president had his aides write his speeches.


Examples of passive causative with the verb have :

1. I have the floor mopped (by the maid).

2. She has her hair dyed (by the hairdresser).

3. My mother had her shopping carried (by the man).

4. The president had his speeches written (by his aides).

36. terdiri dari apa sajakah causative have itu


Causative verb menunjukkan bahwa seseorang/sesuatu secara tidak langsung bertanggung jawab terhadap sebuah tindakan. Subjek tidak melakukan tindakan itu sendiri, tetapi justru menyebabkan seseorang/sesuatu yang lain melakukannya. Contoh:
- Yesterday I had my hair cut.
Pembicara pada kalimat diatas tidak memotong sendiri rambutnya, tetapi justru membuat orang lain melakukannya – Saya “menyebabkan” mereka memotong rambut saya.
Have
Have merupakan causative verb yang umum. Ketimbang melakukan sesuatu dengan diri kita sendiri, kita “menyuruh” orang yang lain untuk melakukannya. Bentuknya sebagai berikut:
Kata kerja “to have” + objek + past participle (verb 3). Contoh:
- I had my jacket cleaned yesterday.
- Did you have your computer fixed?
Terkadang kita menggunakan have sebagai causative verb ketika kita ingin melakukan tindakan oleh diri kita sendiri. Contoh:
- When will the report be ready? I’ll do it by tomorrow morning. >>
- When will the report be ready? I’ll have it done by tomorrow morning.
Dengan menggunakan causative, kalimat ke-dua diatas mengalihkan perhatian dari pelaku tindakan, dan lebih memberikan perhatian kepada tindakan yang sedang dilakukan. Ini kedengaran sopan dan profesional.
Get
Get sering digunakan ketimbang have. Contoh:
• I got my computer fixed – I had my computer fixed. Kedua kalimat ini maknanya sama.
• I got my jacket cleaned. – I had my jacket cleaned. Kedua kalimat ini maknanya sama.
Causative verbs sering digunakan bersama dengan pengalaman-pengalaman negatif. Pada situasi-situasi ini lebih umum menggunakan have. Contoh:
• I had my wallet stolen. (Saya sebenarnya tidak menyebabkan dompet saya dicuri – seseorang mencuri dompet saya dariku)
• She had her window smashed.
