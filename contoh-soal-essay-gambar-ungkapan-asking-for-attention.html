2. Buat contoh ungkapan sebanyak 3 buah a. Showing attention and asking for attention

Attention please

Penjelasan:

Asking for attention :

Look! (Lihat!).

Attention, please. (Mohon perhatian).

May I have your attention, please? (Bolehkah saya meminta perhatianmu?).

Your attention, please. (Mohon perhatian).

Excuse me. (Permisi).

Sorry to bother you. (Maaf mengganggumu).

Showing attention :

Oh, I see. (Oh, begitu).

Is that all? (Apakah itu saja?).

Tell me more about it. (Katakan padaku lebih banyak tentang itu).

Oh my God! What happens next? (Oh Tuhan! Apa yang terjadi selanjutnya?).

What’s next? (Bagaimana selanjutnya?).

Really? (Sungguh?).

18. contoh ungkapan tentang asking and giving attention,checking for understand,showing appereciation,asking and giving opinion​


Jawaban:

Contoh ungkapan yang berkaitan dengan aspek-aspek komunikasi dalam berbicara antara lain:

1. Asking and giving attention (meminta dan memberikan perhatian):

- Can you please listen to me for a moment?

- I hope you don't mind, but I really need someone to talk to right now.

- Sorry to interrupt, but I think what I have to say is important.

2. Checking for understanding (memastikan pemahaman):

- Do you understand what I'm saying so far?

- Am I explaining this clearly enough?

- Can you repeat back to me what you think I said?

3. Showing appreciation (menunjukkan apresiasi):

- Thank you so much for listening to me.

- I really appreciate your input on this.

- Your support means the world to me.

4. Asking and giving opinion (meminta dan memberikan pendapat):

- What do you think about this situation?

- I'm interested in hearing your opinion on this matter.

- Can you give me some feedback on my idea?

20. 5 SOAL TENTANG EXPRESSIONS FOR ASKING ATTENTION


Expressing of Asking attention

When you have to deliver announcement in front of class you need to express the expression to make you become the centre of attention. Below are some common expression used to make the class listen to your speech or announcement. 

1. May I have your attention, please 
2. Can I get the attention! Thanks 
3. Excuse me! 
4. Attention please! 
5. Stop talking please… 
6. Everybody, may I have your attention, please ?


21. Buat contoh ungkapan sebanyak 3 buah a. Showing attention and asking for attention b. Showing understending checking for understending


Jawaban:

checking

Penjelasan:

checking for understanding :

Do you understand what I’m saying? (Apakah kamu mengerti apa yang saya katakan?).

Do you know what I mean? (Apakah kamu tahu maksud saya?).

Do you know what I’m saying? (Apakah kamu tahu apa yang aku katakan?).

Do you know what I’m talking about? (Apakah kamu tahu apa yang aku bicarakan?).

Do you get my point? (Apakah kamu paham maksudku?).

Do you understand? (Apakah kamu paham?).

Showing understanding :

I understand what you are saying. (Aku paham dengan apa yang kamu katakan).

I know what you mean. (Aku tahu apa yang kamu maksud).

I see what you are saying. (Aku mengerti apa yang kamu ucapkan).

I see what you mean. (Aku mengerti apa yang kamu maksud).

I know what you are talking about. (Saya tahu apa yang kamu bicarakan).

I know. (Aku tahu).
