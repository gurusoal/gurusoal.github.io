After getting the food, we went to the nocturnal house where we saw birds and reptiles which only come out at night.

11. Recount text itu kayak gimana ?? kelas 8 !!


recount text bisa diartikan sebagai "Text yang menceritakan"
Generic structure (susunan umum) recount text ini terdiri dari: orientation, events, reorientation.
Contoh Recount Text (1) tentang Masa SMP
My Adolescence
I had my adolescence when I was thirteen.
It started with acne that showed up on my face. It was very annoying. It lowered my self-esteem and I was embarrassed to come out of my house and play with friends. Fortunately, my Mum gave me a good medicine. In three weeks, the acnes started to vanish although those showed some black spots in my face.
That was my bad experience with adolescence, though there were still lots of good experience too.
Analisa :
Orientation : Paragraf pertama, (I had my adolescence when I was thirteen) Events : Paragraf kedua dan ketiga. Reorientation : Paragraf keempat (terakhir)


Recount text itu untuk menceritakan kembali kejadian di masa lampau,information (lampau), atau pengalaman.
Karena masa lampau jadi menggunakan tenses:Simple past, Past continuous, atau Past perfect.

1. Orientation

I spent my last summer holiday in Seoul, South Korea. I went there with my friends

2.Events

On the first day, I was landed at Incheon Airport around 7 AM after a 6 hours long flight. Then I went to Seoul by train and checked in to the hotel I already booked. I decided to take a rest for a while. At night, I went to Hongdae, a famous district in South Korea. I went to eat traditional Korean food. On the next day, I went to the Gyeongbokgung Palace and National Museum. I also went to learn how to make Kimchi and see the scenery of Seoul from Seoul Tower. I went back to the hotel at 10 PM and immediately went to sleep.

3.Reorientation

I spent my last day in Myeongdong and bought some stuffs and souvenirs for my family and friends. I also ate the street food there. I went to the airport at 3 PM because my flight was at 5 PM. I had a fantastic experience in South Korea and made wonderful memory with my friends. It was a great trip.

Last Wednesday was a tiring day. I spent my time to do a lot of activities, that I had no time to take a rest.

32. contoh Recount text tentang pengalaman di sekolah smp


The Amazing School Trip To Bali

When I was in Junior High School, my classmates and I went to Bali for a school trip. We had to take a bus from Madiun to Bali. It took us almost the whole day to get to Bali. However, the bus trip was so much fun because my classmates were very entertaining.

After we arrived in Bali, we went straight to the Monkey Temple. In that place, we could see a lot of cheeky yet cute monkeys. One monkey managed to steal my friend's water bottle. It was hilarious! I couldn't stop laughing at him.

Next, we went to Nusa Dua Beach. My friends and I decided to ride "banana boat". After trying to bargain for half hour with the vendor, we finally managed to get a decent price. The banana boat turned out to be the coolest thing we did in Bali. We rode a boat that looked like a banana, and it was pulled by a boat. The boat kept on making a U turn quickly until we are all fell off the boat. It was such a great experience!

Finally, it was time to end the trip. We had to say goodbye to Bali. I felt so sad but I couldn't really do anything. However, I knew I will go back to Bali again one day. With that thought in mind, I finally was able to get on the bus and went back to Madiun.

Hope this helps!
