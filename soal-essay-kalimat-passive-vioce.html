1. anton sells some candies, kalimat passive viocenya bagaimana?


some candies are sold by anton
passive voice --> simple present tense :
rumus : S + To be (am, is, are) + Verb 3 + by + O
Anton sells some candies. (active)
some candies are sold by Anton. (passive)

2. Contoh teks pembuatan makanan menggunakan passive vioce


Passive voice membuat suatu objek suatu kalimat menjadi subjek dari kalimat tersebut.

S + to be + V3 + Predicate

1. Ketchup is made of soybean.
(Kecap seharusnya adalah objek tetapi karena menggunakan passive voice, maka "kecap" menjadi subjek nya)

2. Hamburger was consisted of buns, sausage, tomatoes, cheese, etc.

11. Ubah ke passive vioce 1 mr edy will sign the contract agreement 2 the police anversted the thieves 3 no body assist me to finish my work


MAPEL : BAHASA INGGRIS
MATERI : PASSIVE VOICE


SOAL DAN PEMBAHASAN
===========================
1). Mr. edy will sign the contract agreement. (Simple future)
Passive : The contract agreement will be signed by Mr. Edy

Rumus passive simple future :
Subject + Will + be + V3 + by... + Object/complement

2). The police arrested the thieves. (simple past)
Passive : The thieves were arrested by the police.

Rumus passive simple past :
Subject + Tobe (was/were) + V3 + By.. + Object/complement


3). Nobody assist me to finish my work. (Simple present)
Passive : I am assisted to finish my work by myself.

Rumus passive :
Subject + tobe (am/are/is) + V3 + By.. + Object/complement

18. Ubah ke passive vioce 1 i will take My son to the zoo next sunday 2 Ida has asked me several questions


MAPEL : BAHASA INGGRIS
MATERI : CHANGE INTO PASSIVE VOICE

SOAL DAN PEMBAHASAN
===========================

1). I will take My son to the zoo next sunday. (simple future)
Passive : My son will be taken to the zoo by me next Sunday

Rumus passive simple future :
Subject + will + be + V3 + By... + Object/complement

2). Ida has asked me several questions. (Present perfect)
Passive : I have been asked several questions by Ida.

Rumus passive present perfect :
Subject + Have/Has + Been + V3 + By... + Object/complement
