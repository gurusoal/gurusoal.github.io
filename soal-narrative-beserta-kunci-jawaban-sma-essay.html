Snow White

Once upon a time there lived a little girl named Snow White. She lived with her aunt and uncle because her parents were dead.

One day she heard her uncle and aunt talking about leaving Snow White in the castle because they both wanted to go to America and they didn’t have enough money to take Snow White.

Snow White didn’t want her uncle and aunt to do that so she decided that it would be best if she ran away. The next morning she ran away into the woods. She was very tired and hungry. Then she saw a little cottage. She knocked but no one answered so she went inside and fell asleep.

Meanwhile, the seven dwarfs were coming home from work. There they found Snow White sleeping. Then Snow White woke up. She saw the dwarfs.

The dwarfs said, “What is your name?”

Snow White said, “My name is Snow White.”

Then, Snow White told the dwarfs the whole story.

The dwarfs said, “If you want, you may live here with us.”

Snow White answered, “Oh, could I? Thank you.”

Finally, Snow White and the seven dwarfs lived happily ever after.

19. contoh soal narrative tex beserta jawabannya


Narrative Text

JAWABAN:

Cinderella

Once upon a time there was a beautiful girl called Cinderella. She lived with her step sister and step mother. They were very bossy. She had to do all the house work.

One day, an invitation to the ball came to the family. Her step sister did not let her to go, so Cinderella was very sad. The step sister went to the ball without her.

Fortunately, her fairy good mother came and helped her to get to the ball. At the ball, Cinderella danced with the prince. The prince fell in love with her then he married her. They lived happily ever after.  

Questions:

What type of text is used by the writer? Narrative textWhat is the communicative purpose of the text above? The communicative purpose of the text above is entertain the readers with a fairy tale.What is the generic structure of the text above? Orientation, complication, resolution and reorientation.What tense is mostly used in the text? Simple past tenseHow did Cinderella go the ball? Her fairy good mother came and helped her to get to the ball.

PEMBAHASAN:

Narrative text adalah teks yang bertujuan  untuk menghibur dan menarik minat pembaca dengan menyajikan cerita atau peristiwa yang memiliki masalah yang menimbulkan konflik dan pada akhir cerita ada resolusinya atau akhir yang bahagia atau bahkan menyedihkan. Sebenarnya teks narrative tidak hanya terbatas pada cerita yang berbau mistis, fiksi, legenda, dongeng ataupun fabel saja, tapi cerita lain yang berbentuk petualangan, misteri dan semua bentuk cerita. Intinya, narrative text adalah tentang cerita.

20. 5 soal essay tentang puisi beserta jawabannya​


Jawaban:

1. Jelaskan tentang puisi soneta !

Jawaban:

Soneta yaitu puisi yang terdiri dari 14 baris yang dibagi menjadi dua, dimana dua bait pertama masing-masing 4 baris, dan dua bait kedua masing-masing tiga baris. Soneta merupakan puisi paling terkenal karena terkesan susah untuk diciptakan. Namun, hal tersebut justru menjadi tantangan tersendiri bagi para penyair.

2.Apa yang kamu ketahui tentang puisi kontemporer!

Jawaban:

Puisi kontemporer adalah jenis puisi yang berusah keluar dari ikatan konvensional. Dalam isinya, puisi ini selalu berusaha menyesuaikan dengan perkembangan zaman dan tidak lagi mementingkan irama, gaya bahasa dan lain-lainnya yang terdapat dalam puisi lama maupun baru.

3.Tuliskan pengertian puisi mantra!

Jawaban:

Puisi mantra adalah puisi yang mengambil sifat-sifat dari mantra.

4.Tuliskan pengertian puisi konkret!

Jawaban:

Puisi konkret adalah puisi yang lebih mengutamakan bentuk grafis (wajah dan bentuk lainnya) dan tidak sepenuhnya menggunakan bahasa sebagai media.

5.Tuliskan pengertian puisi lama!

Jawaban:

Puisi lama adalah puisi yang dihasilkan sebelum abad ke-20. Jenis ini terbagi kedalam beberapa jenis pula, diantaranya adalah pantun, talibun, pantun berkait (seloka), pantun kilat (karmina), gurindam, syair, mantra dll

Narrative Text

In the forest the fastest running animal is the rabbit. No one can beat him. The rabbit arrogantly posed a challenge to all forest dwellers to be able to defeat him.

When none of the other animals dared to accept his challenge, suddenly the turtle, which was known to walk very slowly, said he had accepted the challenge. One forest in unison laughed at him in disbelief. Moreover, the rabbit who was so arrogant underestimated the ability of the turtle.

On the day of the start of the match the rabbit began to show his ability to run very fast at the beginning of the match. The turtle began to walk quietly and slowly, as if ignoring the ability and arrogance of the rabbit.

The rabbit because he felt that he had the upper hand after running a long distance, he walked leisurely, then sat under a tree. The very cool wind apparently lulled the rabbit to sleep.

Apparently the turtle although walking very slowly but he never stopped. Until finally he passed the rabbit who was asleep under a tree.

Finally the rabbit woke up and chased the turtle, but unfortunately his efforts were in vain, because the turtle had already reached the finish line and became the winner. The rabbit realized his arrogance.

The fable above gives a message to us that high ability if not accompanied by humility will only produce arrogance. On the other hand, ordinary abilities but accompanied by perseverance will definitely produce real results.
