4. buatlah soal past tense + past perfect (5),past tense + past continous(5)


A. PAST TENSE VS PAST CONTINUOUS
1. My mother called me last night, when I was studying
2. The children were watching television, when their parents came home
3. You were making dinner, when my friends went home
4. When she was cleaning the windows, her husband called me
5. I was sleeping, when my brother woke me up

Note :
Gunakan PAST CONTINUOUS pada kalimat atau kejadian yang terjadi lebih dulu. Dalam bahasa Indonesia bisa diartikan "..... SEDANG MELAKUKAN".
Baik PAST TENSE dan PAST CONTINUOUS digunakan untuk kalimat yang sudah terjadi di masa lampau

B. PAST TENSE VS PAST PERFECT
1. When she was 21 years old, she had married
2. He had a luxury car, before he was 30 years old
3. They had had lunch, before they went to the library
4. Before we watched television, we had had done our homework
5. After they had gone to Paris, they went to England

Note :
Gunakan PAST PERFECT pada kalimat atau kejadian yang sudah selesai dikerjakan, dan dilanjutkan kejadian selanjutnya dimana kalimatnya menggunakan SIMPLE PAST. Dalam bahasa Indonesia, PAST PERFECT bisa diartikan ".... SUDAH MELAKUKAN".

Dimana Latihannya? Saya Jelaskan Materinya Yah Semoga Paham ^^

Past tense is the tense which is used when we want to express something that happened or was done in the past time. The Simple Past tense → actions that have already taken place in the past.

Pendahuluan:

Past tense is the tense which is used when we want to express something that happened or was done in the past time. The Simple Past tense → actions that have already taken place in the past.

Formula :

A. Helping Verb [ With Example ]

(+) S + V² + O = I Was a Teacher

(?) V² + S + O = Was I a Teacher ?

(-) S + V² + Not + O = I Was not a Teacher

B. Full Verb [ With Example ]

(+) S + V² + O = She Drew an Apple

(?) Did + S + V¹ + O = Did She Draw an Apple?

(-) S + did + not + V¹ + O = She Did not Draw an Apple

Notes :

Keterangan waktu yang biasa dipakai adalah yesterday ( kemarin ) , last ( lalu ) , dan ago ( lalu ).

Untuk V² dibagi menjadi 2 jenis yaitu :

1) Regular Verbs ( Kata Kerja Beraturan )

Catatan : adding "d" or "ed"

Open = OpenedStudy = Studied

2. Irregular Verbs ( Kata Kerja Non Beraturan )

Catatan : not adding "d" or "ed"

Draw = DrewGo = Went

Untuk membaca V² yang berakhiran "ed" pada kata kerja Regular Verbs adalah dengan cara menekan ujung lidah tanpa harus dikeluarkan/membunyikannya secara jelas.

Pembahasan:

The Simple Past tense → actions that have already taken place in the past. V² dibagi menjadi 2 yaitu
