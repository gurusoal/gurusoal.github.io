34. Dialog certainty,doubt,suprises,repetaiton jadi satu dialog


Kinoshita: Hey, Saeki! How are you?
Saeki: I'm sorry, could you repeat your question again?
Kinoshita: Okay. I was asking how are you?
Saeki: Oh, I'm fine, don't worry.
Kinoshita: Are you sure? You look a bit pale.
Saeki: Don't worry, I'm fine.
Kinoshita: Okay, if you say so.
Saeki: Also, have you heard about Maki?
Kinoshita: Hm? What is it?
Saeki: I heard that Maki is sick.
Kinoshita: What? I doubt it! Yesterday, I talked with Maki and she was fine.
Saeki: Oh, okay.
Kinoshita: Speaking about Maki, she told me that her sister was so sick. She had to he hospitalized.
Saeki: Oh my God! Will she be okay?
Kinoshita: Don't worry, Maki said her sister's condition is getting better.
Saeki: Oh, thank God. I hope she will be completely healthy soon.
Kinoshita: Me too.

KETERANGAN
1. Saeki: I'm sorry, could you repeat your question again? = Repetition
2. Saeki: Don't worry. I'm fine. = Certainty
3. Kinoshita: What? I doubt it! Yesterday, I talked with Maki and she was fine. = Doubt
4. Saeki: Oh my God! Will she be okay? = Surprise
