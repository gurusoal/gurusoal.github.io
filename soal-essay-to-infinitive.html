21. Subject+verb+enough+noun+to infinitive Berikan 2 contoh Subject+verb+adjective+enough+to infinitive Berikan 2 contoh Subject+verb+too+adjective+to infinitive Berikan 2 contoh


Sub-category : Too vs Enough


Level : JHS


Code : 5


Keywords : too, enough, adjective


-------------


Too dan enough digunakan untuk menerangkan adjective, adverb, atau noun.  

Too and enough menyatakan degree.



Too berarti "terlalu". Too memiliki arti negatif.


Too digunakan sebelum adjective atau adverb.


Contoh : He is too young to go to school.



Enough berarti "cukup".  

Enough diletakkan setelah adjective atau adverb.


Contoh : He is old enough to go to school.



Enough diletakkan sebelum kata benda.


Contoh : I have enough money.


-----------------


Subject+verb+enough+noun+to infinitive

I have enough money to buy the car.

She made enough food to feed all the guests.

---------------

Subject+verb+adjective+enough+to infinitive

He seems strong enough to lift the heavy rock.

They feel angry enough to call the police.

--------------

Subject+verb+too+adjective+to infinitive

The film sounds too mature to be watched by young children.

The soup tastes too horrible to be served to the food critic.

38. Put the verb in brackets in correct forms either using "gerund, infinitive or to Infinitive"​


Jawaban:

Put the verb in brackets in correct form, either using "gerund, infinitive, or to infinitive"!

1. I hate seeing/to see you working so hard.

2. Will you advise her to stop smoking so much?

3. Is he going to keep telling me that he is right?

4. They enjoy dancing until late at night.

5. Don't ever let me find you day-dreaming.

6. I must ask you to stop interfering.

7. You are supposed to obey my order without asking any more questions.

8. Could you manage to finish packing your suitcases by 7 o'clock?

9. I can not stop loving you, sweet-heart!

10. May I ask you to begin eating/to eat now without waiting for the others to come?

Pembahasan

Gerund

Gerund sama dengan Verba + ing, namun jenis kata (part of speech) nya bukan sebagai kata kerja, tapi kata benda. Sebagai kata benda, gerund dapat digunakan sebagai subjek/objek, ataupun objek preposisi dalam kalimat.

Gerund, jika digunakan sebagai objek, hanya setelah beberapa kata kerja (hate, stop, keep, enjoy, find, finish, begin, dll.). Contoh kalimat:      I enjoy doing my homework in the library.Gerund, jika digunakan sebagai objek preposisi, selalu muncul setelah preposisi (at, in, on, over, about, from, for, without, by, with, against, through, dll.) Contoh kalimat: We are talking about preparing a party for her.

To + infinitive

Sama seperti gerund, to + infinitive/verba 1 juga bisa digunakan sebagai subjek/objek atau setelah kata sifat

Jika digunakan sebagai objek, to + infinitive juga hanya muncul setelah beberapa kata kerja (hate, advise, ask, manage, stop, begin, wait, dll.) Contoh kalimat: Messi managed to score a goal in the last minutes. Jika digunakan setelah kata sifat, hanya diikuti oleh beberapa kata sifat (glad, happy, supposed, pleased, lucky, fortunate, ashamed, dll.)

        Contoh kalimat: I'm happy to see them playing together.

Catatan tambahan

Ada beberapa kata kerja yang dapat diikuti oleh baik gerund maupun to + infinitive (hate, begin, stop, continue, forget, like, love, prefer, regret, remember, start, and try). Beberapa kata kerja jika diikuti oleh gerund/to + infinitive tidak memiliki perubahan makna, namun pada sebagian lainnya maknanya berubah. Contoh:

         He began singing on the stage. (Dia mulai bernyanyi di panggung.)  

         He began to sing on the stage. (Dia mulai bernyanyi di panggung.)

   She stops calling her mother. (kebiasaan menelepon berhenti)

  She stops to call her mother. (berhenti sejenak untuk menelepon ibunya)

Penerapan:

1. I                   hate (see)                     you (work) so hard.

      gerund/to + inf) karena hate        gerund karena see  

  Jawaban: I hate seeing/to see you working so hard.

2. Will you advise her (stop)                         (smoke) so much?

               to + inf karena advise        gerund karena stop & arti kebiasaan

 Jawaban: Will you advise her to stop smoking so much?

3. Is he going (keep)          (tell) me that he is right?

   (be going to + inf)          gerund karena keep

   Jawaban: Is he going to keep telling me that he is right?

4. They enjoy (dance) until late at night.

              gerund karena enjoy

    Jawaban: They enjoy dancing until late at night.

5. Don't every let me (find) you                     (day-dream).

                          find karena let             gerund karena causative let

    Jawaban: Don't ever let me find you day-dreaming.

6. I must (ask) you                          (stop)                           (interfere).

  ask karena modal must     to + inf karena ask        gerund karena stop

  Jawaban: I must ask you to stop interfering.

7. You are supposed (obey) my order without (ask) any more questions.

                     to + inf karena supposed        gerund karena preposisi without

Jawaban: You are supposed to obey my order without asking any more questions.

8. Could you (manage)               (finish)    (pick) your suitcase by 7 o'clock?

manage karena modal could    to+inf karena manage  gerund karena finish          

Jawaban: Could you manage to finish packing your suitcases by 7 o'clock?

9. I can not (stop)                         (love) you, sweet-heart!

   stop karena modal can           gerund karena stop & arti kebiasaan

Jawaban: I can not stop loving you, sweet-heart!

10. May I ask you 1(begin) 2(eat) now without 3(wait) for the others 4(come)?

1. to + inf karena ask      

2. (to + inf/gerund) karena begin

3. gerund karena without

4. to + inf karena wait
